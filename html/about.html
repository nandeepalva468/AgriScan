About Us

Agriculture is a cornerstone of livelihood in India, engaging more than 55% of the population. With over 60% of the workforce involved in farming, India stands as the second-largest farm output nation globally.

As agriculture generates vast amounts of data daily, leveraging intelligent diagnostic and analytic techniques can significantly boost both productivity and profitability. This system empowers farmers with insights to select the best-suited crops for their regions, supporting smarter decisions for both individuals and governments.
